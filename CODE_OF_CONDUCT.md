# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and communicate clearly.
- Provide actionable feedback focused on ideas, not individuals.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors who violate this code.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to repository maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
